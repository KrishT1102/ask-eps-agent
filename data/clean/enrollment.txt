Enrollment - Everett Public Schools
Welcome to Everett Public Schools! We are proud to serve students at all grade levels, offering a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and supportive resources to help every student thrive. Enrolling with us opens the door to a vibrant learning community where curiosity, growth, and success are encouraged every step of the way.
We are excited to help you get your child enrolled with our easy Online Enrollment system. You can use any computer or mobile device to complete the process, which takes about 30 minutes. Our secure online enrollment system makes registering your child simple and convenient. Submit forms, upload documents, and track your progress - all in one place.

You can upload scanned or photographed copies of your required documents during online registration. This can save you a trip to the school office.
If you do not have electronic copies of your documents, you can still register online; you will just need to bring your documents to the school to complete your enrollment submission. It is helpful if you are using your phone to enroll online to take pictures of your documents with your phone before you begin.
1. Proof of Address: Acceptable documents include
Current utility bill,
homeowner's policy, etc.
The document must show the parent/guardian's name and address.  Documents should also be dated within the last 6 months.
2. Proof of Age: Required for Kindergarten or Grade 1 students who have not been previously enrolled in a US school:
A religious, hospital, or physician's certificate showing date of birth
Previously verified school records
An affidavit from a parent
An entry in a family Bible
Or any other documents permitted by law.
3. Certificate of Immunization Status : Must be printed either from WA MyIR or a WAIIS Certificate of Immunization Status (CIS), or a copy signed by a physician.
4. Other Documents : May include but not limited to custody and legal documents, medication orders, prior report cards, withdrawal forms, unofficial transcripts.
For enrollment-related inquiries, please reach out directly to the school.

Everett Public Schools provides full-day, state-funded kindergarten. Families are encouraged to enroll early and attend orientation sessions to help prepare their child and ensure a smooth start to school.
Choice programs offer all students, including those with economic or exceptional needs, access to personalized learning that fits their goals. This includes High School Online Options, Sequoia HS, Dual Language, IB and more.
Everett Public Schools Early Childhood Education and Assistance Program (ECEAP) is a state-funded, free preschool program for qualifying children and families, designed to support early learning, school readiness, and overall child development.
CTE Signature STEM Program offers five industry-led career pathways with certifications, leadership opportunities, scholarships, and free transportation for students attending outside their home high school.
Port Gardner School is a parent partnership program that offers engaging onsite classes with certificated teachers and provides resources, guidance, and support for home-based education.
Special Services Placement
Everett Public Schools offers specialized services for eligible students through a formal referral and evaluation process, ensuring individualized support based on each student’s unique needs.

All children enrolling in kindergarten must be 5 years old by midnight on August 31 of that school year. For more information about early learning programs, go to: Kindergarten webpage
All children enrolling in first grade must be 6 years old by midnight on August 31 of that school year. This is the state uniform entry age.
