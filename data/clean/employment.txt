Start your Future with Us
Everett Public Schools is focused on inspiring students to achieve and thrive. This work is based on our seven CORE VALUES – Respect, Learning, Equity, Collaboration, Passion, Integrity, and Diversity.
Everett Public Schools is a great place to work with 18 elementary, 5 middle, and 4 high schools, and over 2,000 staff members. Serving 20,000+ students speaking 90+ languages, it offers a diverse and supportive community with consistent funding for schools and facilities. Located 25 miles north of Seattle, Everett offers a small-town feel with easy access to city amenities and abundant outdoor activities.

Administrative Staff
Create an Account: For new applicants, start by clicking on the apply button associated to the vacancy you are interested in, this will populate the frontline application page, enter your email address and a password.
If you have an active Frontline account elsewhere, you will be recognized by your email address when you begin our application and given the opportunity to import relevant information.
Complete the Application: The online application form takes about 30-45 minutes. Fields marked with an asterisk are required. Your application cannot be submitted until all required fields are complete. Dates must be entered in the formats shown.
Submit Your Application: Click "Next Page" to progress and "Submit" when finished. You’ll receive a confirmation of successful submission. Remember, you may return to your application to make changes and additions as needed, but you must have your user-id and password.  We recommend that you print a copy of your completed application after submittal for your records
Complete Additional Requirements: The online application is only part of the process. Reference forms are required and will be emailed automatically to your references once the application is submitted.
Only complete applications with all supporting documents including 2 completed confidential references (1 must be from a supervisor) returned via email before the closing date and time will be screened.
