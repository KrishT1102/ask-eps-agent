Payments - Everett Public Schools
Families can conveniently pay for meals, fees, and other school costs online. Secure payment systems accept major credit/debit cards and electronic checks, letting you add funds or make payments anytime without visiting the school office.
Payments Online is an internet web service allowing parents or guardians to make payments for Everett Public Schools purchases, fines or fees. Users have student historical receipts available as well as the ability to pay for student library and textbook fines. On a school by school basis there may also be a limited offering of school and district-sponsored items or classes also available for internet purchase.
To access specific information about your student, your User Name will be your student's district-assigned student id number and the Password will be that same student's registered last name (this must be entered in UPPERCASE letters).
If you have more than one student attending Everett Public Schools, you will log in separately for each one.
Currently, there are no convenience fees for purchases made online. This will be reevaluated in future years. If a refund is issued for a payment where a convenience fee has been charged, the convenience fee will not be refunded.

MySchoolBucks lets families securely pay for student meals, activity fees, and other school-related expenses online or through the mobile app. You can add funds, set up automatic payments, and track balances and purchases anytime.
My School Bucks also has a free downloadable app for Android and Apple users. The free My School Bucks mobile app allows users to view balances, make payments, monitor cafeteria purchase history, transfer funds from sibling to sibling, and receive low balance push notifications, all from the palm of their hand. To download the app, search in the Apple or Play Store and search for My School Bucks or MSB Parents.

You can also pay via cash or checks. Make checks payable to "School Name" or Everett Public Schools. We cannot accept checks without an imprinted name and address. Please write your student's name or ID number in the memo line.
Elementary Only: Please provide cash in a sealed and clearly labeled baggie or envelope with your student's First and Last name and student ID number.
