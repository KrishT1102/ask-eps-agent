Transportation
The Transportation Department ensures safe, reliable, and efficient bus services for all students. We are committed to providing timely transportation, maintaining well-serviced vehicles, and supporting student safety on every trip to and from school and school-related activities.
The district contracts with Durham School Services for school bus drivers and buses. Durham drivers are not district employees, and the buses (with the exception of some special education buses, and vans) are not district property. The Durham contract includes maintenance and repair of school buses, and careful driver safety and training.
Transportation Department Services:
Schedules and routes daily school bus transportation.
Provides buses for vocational education, athletic events, and extracurricular trips.
Coordinates with state and local officials on road construction, new housing developments, and conditions affecting student transportation.

Kindergarten through 12th-grade students who live more than a half-mile walking distance from school can ride a bus. We may make exceptions for students who live where conditions make walking to and from school hazardous.
Eligibility and exceptions are guided by the Washington State Department of Transportation’s School Walk and Bike Routes: A Guide for Planning and Improving Walk and Bike to School Options for Students

Safety Tips
Following school bus safety tips helps keep students safe on and around the bus. Staying aware, following basic rules, and practicing caution ensures a safer and smoother ride for everyone.
Consistently practicing these habits builds good routines that protect both students and drivers.

Snow Routes
During severe weather, buses may operate on designated snow routes to ensure student safety. These routes are planned to avoid hazardous areas and help maintain reliable transportation when conditions are challenging.

My Stop App
The My Stop app lets families track their child’s school bus in real time. It provides estimated arrival times and updates for a safer, more convenient commute.
It also helps parents plan their schedules better and stay informed about any delays or route changes.
