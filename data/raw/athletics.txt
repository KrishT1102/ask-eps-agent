Athletics
​The Everett Public Schools Athletics Department offers comprehensive sports programs for students in grades 7–12, emphasizing teamwork, sportsmanship, and personal growth. Middle schools compete in the Sno-King League, while high schools participate in the Western Conference (Wesco), providing opportunities in sports such as football, basketball, soccer, wrestling, track, and volleyball. The department aims to enrich students' educational experiences through athletics, fostering values that extend beyond the classroom.
