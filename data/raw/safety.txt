Safety Concern
Call 911 if you are in immediate danger. SafeSchools Alert is not monitored 24 hours a day. Any in-progress or life safety event should be reported immediately to 911.
Report concerns about bullying, harassment, drugs, vandalism, or any safety issue quickly and easily with SafeSchools Alert. All reports are reviewed promptly to help maintain a safe and secure learning environment. You can submit a tip in four convenient ways.
Every tip submitted to Vector Alert is taken seriously and will be acted upon. Intentionally submitting a tip that you know to be false is a criminal offense.
You can now submit tips via the Vector Alert App for Android or Apple smartphones. Choose the corresponding app store below to download the app.
To submit tips to your organization, enter the following code in the app when prompted.  #1350
Thank you in advance for submitting your tip and doing your part to help make our school community an even safer place to work and learn.

Suicide Prevention
Help is available. You can reach out for help for yourself or to support someone else. If you are in crisis, you can call the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline 24 hours a day, seven days a week, by calling 9-8-8.

Crisis Support
