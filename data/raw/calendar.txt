District Calendar
Calendar Note: At this time, Everett Public Schools is planning for students in grades 1-12 to start the 2026-27 school year on Wednesday, September 2, 2026. Kindergarten students would begin school the following week, on Tuesday, September 8, 2026.
These dates are not final. The official student calendar is part of collective bargaining, which is expected to occur over the summer.
